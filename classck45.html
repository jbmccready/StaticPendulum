Detailed Description

Cash and Karp embedded Runge Kutta order 5(4) adaptive step integrator.

See: J. R. Cash, A. H. Karp. “A Variable Order Runge-Kutta Method for Initial Value Problems with Rapidly Varying Right-Hand Sides.” ACM Transactions on Mathematical Software, Vol. 16, No. 3, 1990.

And Wikipedia: http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cash-Karp_method
